About Me

Hi! I'm Gülfem Aytekin, a Software Developer with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from the University of Oslo.

I've been an avid (human) language learner from a young age. When I was trying to decide what career path to take, I couldn't think of anything else but something where my language learning enthuisiasm and skills would come in handy. That's how I ended up as a Software Developer. Having taken not only computer science courses about object-oriented programming, the Semantic Web, natural language processing, etc. but also linguistics related courses in English, German and French at my university, I have gotten to indulge in both human languages and programming languages.

I still love human languages, am excited to learn more of them on my own time and possibly even get to incorporate my linguistic skills into my career as a software developer some day. For now, I am open to trying any kind of software development.

Currently, I am working on a hobby project, a tarot reading machine. I have been painting a deck of tarot cards in my free time, and thought it would be awesome to create a program that does tarot readings, using the cards I have painted for the visuals. That way, my developer self gets to collaborate with my hobby-artist self. I have written a simple program that draws a random card and displays both the card and the message. I am not yet done painting all the cards, and I am looking to write a more complex program when I have the time. For now, feel free to draw from a limited amount of cards!
